Meet Our Founder

Shahzada Naeem Bokhari is a distinguished Advocate of the Supreme Court of Pakistan and a renowned media personality with a legal career spanning over five decades. He earned his Bachelor of Arts from Government College University, Lahore, and an LL.B. with first‑division honors from Punjab University Law College. Early in his career, he practiced under prominent lawyers, including S. M. Zafar, and honed expertise in civil, constitutional, labor, and industrial law. In 1980, he established Legal Advisers Inc., with offices in Lahore and Karachi. Over the years, Mr. Bokhari has represented high‑profile clients, including Pakistan Steel Mills, Pakistan Railways, National Bank of Pakistan, Attock Oil Company, and OGDC, and has even advised international bodies, such as the World Bank, on labor issues.

Beyond his legal practice, Mr. Bokhari is a recognized face in the media. He served as host of Geo News’s popular political satire show Khabarnaak, after succeeding Aftab Iqbal, and has anchored other programs like Apnay Andaaz Se and Naeem Bokhari ke Saath ￼. In the political arena, he played a significant legal role in Imran Khan’s historic Panama Papers case, joining PTI formally in June 2016 ￼. In November 2020, he was appointed as the Chairman of Pakistan Television Corporation’s Board, serving as an independent director in recognition of his legal and media background.
